Spotify and Youtube on the same page.
Help

You first need to log in with your spotify account.

In the search results, Play directly plays the song while Queue & Play adds it to the queue and jumps to it (assuming queue was empty before). Finally, when playing a Youtube video, Spotify gets paused. When video ends or when you close it, Spotify playback will resume if it was playing before (assuming the device did not go offline).
